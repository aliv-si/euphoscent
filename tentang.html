About Euphoscènt

Euphoscènt is a local perfume brand born from the passion of providing unique fragrances for every individual. We believe that scent can evoke memories and enhance self-confidence.

Our Story

In 2020, from a cozy kitchen in Yogyakarta, Euphoscènt was born as a simple experiment in fragrance. Since then, we’ve grown to share our signature scents with hundreds who seek beauty in every breath.

The Euphoscènt Dream

To be the first choice of quality perfume in Indonesia that reflects the personality of each user.

What We Do

  • Crafting fragrances with only the finest ingredients
  • Ensuring exceptional quality in scent, packaging, and service
  • Delivering an elegant and trustworthy shopping experience
